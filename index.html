Tic-Tac-Toe Game Instructions

  1. Select the Game Mode:
    • Click "1 Player" to play against the computer.
    • Click "2 Players" to play with a friend.
  2. Understand the Objective:
    • Players take turns to mark an empty cell in a 3x3 grid.
    • The first player to align three of their symbols (X or O) horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.
    • If all cells are filled without any winning alignment, the game ends in a draw.
  3. Start Playing:
    • Player 1 (X) always starts the game.
    • In 1 Player mode, after you make your move, the computer (O) will play automatically.
    • In 2 Players mode, take turns clicking on the grid cells.
  4. Winning or Drawing:
    • If a player aligns three symbols, the game announces the winner in the instructions section.
    • If the game ends in a draw, it will display "This Game is a draw."
  5. Reset the Game:
    • Click the Reset button to clear the board and start a new game.
    • You can also switch between 1 Player and 2 Players modes by clicking the respective buttons.

Tips: In 1 Player mode, the computer moves randomly, so strategize your moves to win! Focus on blocking your opponent's winning combinations while forming your own.
